Detailed Description

Material-dependent data for Urban MSC.

UrbanMsc material data (see UrbanMscParams::calc_material_data) is a set of precalculated material dependent parameters used in sampling the angular distribution of MSC, , and in the step limiter. The coeffient vectors are used in polynomial evaluation.
