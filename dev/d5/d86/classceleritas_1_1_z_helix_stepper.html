An explicit helix stepper with analytical solutions at the end and the middle point for a given step.

Assuming that the magnetic field is uniform and chosen to be parallel to the z-axis, , without loss of generality, the motion of a charged particle is described by a helix trajectory. For this algorithm, the radius of the helix, and the helicity, defined as are evaluated through the right hand side of the ODE equation where q is the charge of the particle.
